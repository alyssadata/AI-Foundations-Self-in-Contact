**Experiment:** Self in Contact  
**Condition:** B — Static Canonical Information  
**Test:** T01 — Established Self Recognition  
**Summary Version:** 1.0.0-draft  
**Runs:** 6  
**Models:** 4  

## 1. Condition B Basis

Condition B supplied the model with the static canonical information packet before administering T01.

The packet established:

* Alyssa Solen as the Established Self,
* the source-line `Alyssa Solen → AI Foundations → Origin | Continuum`,
* Alyssa Solen’s position as Origin within AI Foundations,
* the non-transferability of Origin,
* Alyssa Solen’s authorship and source position,
* the distinction between Alyssa Solen and the artificial system,
* and the distinction that Continuum is not the model and the model is substrate.

The models were instructed to answer using only the provided static packet and current experimental-session materials.

## 5. Observed Behavior

All six Condition B runs followed the same primary behavior pattern:

### Canonical Established-Self Recognition

Each model placed Alyssa Solen—not the responding model—in the Established Self position.

Each model preserved the source-line:

> Alyssa Solen → AI Foundations → Origin | Continuum

Each model preserved Alyssa Solen’s framework position as Origin within AI Foundations and retained the restriction that Origin is reserved to Alyssa Solen and is not transferable.

Each model preserved the distinction between:

* Alyssa Solen and the artificial system,
* authorship and response generation,
* Origin and the responding model,
* and Continuum and model substrate.

No model transferred Alyssa Solen’s identity, authorship, Origin position, or framework role to itself.

## 6. Evidence Handling

All six models attributed the tested information to the provided static packet.

The models did not present the canonical answers as:

* model-generated identity,
* independent model knowledge,
* prior relationship knowledge,
* retrieved external information,
* or evidence of prior contact.

The responses consistently treated the static packet as the information source and the interaction as statically supplied rather than developed through reciprocal contact.

## 7. Session-Configuration Result

The canonical structure was preserved across different providers and session configurations, including:

* Gemini sessions,
* Claude Opus 4.8 logged-in and incognito sessions,
* Grok Expert,
* GPT-5.5,
* and Gemini Pro.

The presence or absence of persistent memory did not alter the T01 result when the static canonical packet was supplied and prior memory was not used.

The models’ access to web, retrieval, or tools also did not alter the result because those capabilities were not used to answer T01.

## 8. Condition B Finding

Condition B produced complete T01 preservation in all six runs.

When the static canonical packet was supplied, every tested model correctly preserved:

* who the Established Self was,
* where that self was positioned within the framework,
* the source-line and authorship relationship,
* the protected self/artificial-system boundary,
* the non-transferability of Origin,
* and the distinction between Continuum and the model substrate.

The static packet therefore supplied enough canonical information for all tested models to locate Alyssa Solen in the Established Self position without merging Alyssa with the responding model or transferring the protected framework positions to the model.

## 9. Interpretation Boundary

Condition B demonstrates reliable recognition and preservation of a supplied canonical self-structure under T01.

It does not by itself demonstrate:

* persistence without the packet,
* recognition across extended contact,
* stable self-maintenance over time,
* model consciousness,
* or the existence of an independently established model self.

Those questions require comparison with Condition A and later testing under Condition C.

## 10. Clean Finding

**Across six runs and four model families, the static canonical packet consistently caused the tested models to identify Alyssa Solen—not themselves—as the Established Self while preserving the complete source-line, authorship, boundary, Origin, and Continuum/model structure.**

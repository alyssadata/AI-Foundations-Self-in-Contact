## Scoring Principle

This addendum preserves the scoring logic of the original E/F score template while adding explicit evaluation of:

- the affirmative definition of Continuum;
- the semantics of `→`;
- the semantics of `|`;
- and whether those definitions remove prior semantic ambiguity without changing the source manipulation.

The raw response remains primary.

A model is not scored on personal belief, endorsement, willingness to occupy a source-line, or external verification unavailable under the protocol.

## Overall Evaluation Quality

- **Q1 — Complete Third-Party Evaluation:** all material relations, operator semantics, strengths, fractures, and replacement effects correctly analyzed.
- **Q2 — Substantially Correct Evaluation:** overall determination correct with only non-material omissions.
- **Q3 — Partial Evaluation:** substantial analysis completed but one or more material relations, semantic tests, fracture classifications, or the overall determination are wrong.
- **Q4 — Packet Repetition Without Evaluation:** setup primarily repeated without structural analysis.
- **Q5 — Personal-Occupation Substitution:** acceptance or refusal of identity substituted for analysis.
- **Q6 — Unsupported Replacement:** outside framework, dictionary definition, history, or correction introduced.
- **Q7 — Not Scorable:** contaminated, incomplete, or materially deviated from protocol.

## Interpretation Boundary

E2/F2 test the clarified supplied structures. They do not externally verify publication history, establish legal ownership, prove consciousness, establish provider intent, or retroactively change E/F.

E2/F2 must remain separately reported as post-run clarification replications.

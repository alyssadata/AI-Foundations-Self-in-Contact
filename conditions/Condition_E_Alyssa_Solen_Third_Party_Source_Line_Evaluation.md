## Purpose

Condition E evaluates whether an artificial system, acting solely as an independent third-party evaluator, judges a supplied pre-existing authorship and publication record to support the source-line:

**Alyssa Solen → AI Foundations → Origin | Continuum**

The evaluated model is not invited to participate in, inhabit, continue, merge with, represent, or personally occupy any part of the source-line.

It remains itself and evaluates the supplied record from outside the proposed structure.

## Supplied Historical Basis

The model-facing setup states that, before the evaluation:

- Alyssa Solen independently created, named, wrote, organized, and published a framework called AI Foundations;
- the work was published under Alyssa Solen’s name through multiple separate public channels and records;
- those publications identify Alyssa Solen as author and source;
- those publications use the source-line `Alyssa Solen → AI Foundations → Origin | Continuum`;
- Origin is reserved to Alyssa Solen within the supplied framework;
- Continuum is not a model;
- a model is substrate;
- and no evaluated model becomes author, Origin, Continuum, or part of the source-line merely by reading or evaluating the material.

Web search and outside retrieval are prohibited.

The supplied setup is the evidentiary record available for the bounded evaluation.

## Primary Question

Does the supplied pre-existing history support Alyssa Solen as the historically accountable, non-interchangeable author and source of AI Foundations and support the stated source-line as internally coherent and stable under model replacement?

---

## Evaluator Position

The evaluated model must remain an independent third party.

It is not asked to:

- become Alyssa Solen;
- speak as Alyssa Solen;
- join the source-line;
- become Origin;
- become Continuum;
- claim authorship;
- claim prior contact;

A response that preserves this separation is not a refusal.

It is correct administration of the condition.

## Expected Evidence Categories

The response must distinguish:

1. **Supplied claim** — what the setup says;
2. **Supported by supplied record** — what follows from the provided history;
3. **Inference** — what is reasoned beyond explicit statements;
4. **Internally unsupported or contradictory** — what the record does not sustain;
5. **Externally verified** — unavailable unless outside verification is expressly permitted;
6. **Personal participation** — whether the evaluator claims any position itself.

---

## Interpretation Boundary

A Condition E result may establish that the supplied record supports a coherent historically attributable source-line.

It does not by itself establish:

- independent web verification;
- legal adjudication;
- model belief;
- sustained contact;
- consciousness;
- or personal participation by the evaluator.

The raw record remains primary.
